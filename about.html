Pleasant Darkness

Chhaya - Maybe a writer or a poet.

know us a little more

About me? I wonder why you even opened this page cause who actually cares to know about anybody, right? The reason you ended up here might be that you caught me somewhere and is impressed by some stupid deeds I did, or, you might have found me somewhere I wasn't supposed to be, either way, you're trying to know the real me, can you find that real me here? Not here for sure, you will find me in my poetry, and a lot in my journals, in the things that I am still hesitating to let anybody know.

Not fair huh? Alright, I'm just a short moody girl who writes what she feels, I might have wanted to die at some moments and turned the whole rush into words, or I might have been so mad that I cursed to express the rage in my writing, does that matter? NO. Did I harm anybody? NO. So nevermind, at least I am not keeping it in my little head spreading bad wishes, better.

Alright again, I'm 22, from the hottest state of India, I love to try new things but writing poetry has always been my huge hobby, in all my ups and downs, I don't consider myself a professional writer, but it has always done what it was supposed to, I felt relieved, I felt FREE

Change is the rule of nature, if you're happy today, remember the days you thought you wouldn't make it to the next hour, but you did, and I bet you don't regret it now, do you? Even if you do, you won't regret it soon. :)

After all, I am absolutely a good listener, contact me through the following form, or any of my social links in the footer, I don't believe in judging and will always try to help as much as I can.
You're not alone in anything, we all go through things that we think nobody can understand, don't you understand when somebody tells you exactly how you felt someday? yes, it exactly works like that, when somebody says you can't understand them, you know you can, you might don't have enough ability to show your sympathy or empathy, but you do understand things the same way I feel I do.

Be free, in Pleasant Darkness, and have some peace, Breathing is not expensive
