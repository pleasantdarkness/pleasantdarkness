Pleasant Darkness

Chhaya- Maybe a writer or a poet.

Pleasant Darkness explains the beauty of darkness within, the darkness that echoes in your mind and whispers in your ears, that feels like a scream that nobody can hear.
I love to write about the extreme flow of emotions the human mind creates, the game of feelings our brain plays within, and the pain the heart holds.
I'm Chhaya, I love to write to ease my mind and I feel that there are many things that our extreme emotions may hold. They can only be understood if expressed, a emotion, a secret, a confession, and a pain if left unsaid, creates a wound that take years to be buried only to drag you again in the six feet hole.
