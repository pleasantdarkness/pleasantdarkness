Pleasant Darkness
-Chhaya

I'm Chhaya, I love to write to ease my mind and I feel that there are many things that our extreme emotions may hold. They can only be understood if expressed, a emotion, a secret, a confession, and a pain if left unsaid, creates a wound that take years to be buried only to drag you again in the six feet hole.
You must be wondering why I chose this name "Pleasant Darkness", there is a long thought behind it, there was a time when I adored the darkness, the peace we get in the dark world, when the world we live in get so cruel that we don't even want to live anymore. The time when we crave the beauty in dark, the peace in chaos, love in pain, and many more unexplainable things.
I can't call myself a poet, or a writer, but writing was something that always calmed me down, helped me to go through my nightmare disorder, anxiety and all the problems that seized my mind for a long time. Writing is something that can help you heal, help you express and help you to stay standing when your mind is so occupied that you can't figure out anything straight.
There are many situations that I faced, many weird things that I felt, many unexplainable moments I lived in, I won't say I had the worst things happened to me, but I believe there are many people out there who are as confused as I was, who might be feeling the same things that they think nobody can understand.
I needed a platform to share how I felt inside, apart from all the things that were going on around, that felt so normal, yet a chaotic war was going on in my mind, that made me restless, week.

At last, I believe that there are many things that I may did wrong, many confessions to be made, many regrets to be burried with time, but every person deserves somebody who can hear them, just giving a ear to somebody who craved a talk can not be a bad idea.
I would appreciate and love to hear your thoughts, confessions, regrets`and all the secrets that occupied your mind, the beautiful or worst experience you had about anything, I would love to hear it all.
